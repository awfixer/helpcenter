## AWOS Sync

AWOS Sync Services provide a way to sync settings from your AWFixerOS Instellation to a remote server. 

AWOS Sync is fully encrypted and secured by the same encryption management system as your account. We never see the keys, and because we never see them even in the unlikely event our servers are compromised your data will be unrecoverable an unusable by the adversary.
